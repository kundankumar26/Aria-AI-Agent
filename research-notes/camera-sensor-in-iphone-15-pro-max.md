# Camera sensor in iPhone 15 Pro Max
_Saved: 2026-05-07T06:37:43.824Z_

Topic: Camera sensor in iPhone 15 Pro Max
Researched: Thursday 7 May, 2026

**Summary**
The iPhone 15 Pro Max uses a multi-sensor rear camera system headlined by a 48 MP main sensor, a 12 MP ultra-wide, and a 12 MP periscope telephoto with 5x optical zoom. It also incorporates a LiDAR depth-sensing system to aid depth mapping and augmented reality features.

**Key Findings**
- 48 MP main camera with high-resolution option: The iPhone 15 Pro Max features a 48 MP main sensor (with a 24 MP default mode) for high-resolution images. (Source: Apple newsroom)
- 5x optical telephoto exclusive to Pro Max: The Pro Max adds a dedicated 5x telephoto lens for optical zoom, exclusive to the Pro Max lineup. (Source: Lux.camera)
- LiDAR depth-sensing system: The Pro models include a LiDAR system to improve depth sensing, corroborated by industry sources and analyses. (Sources: Baiyintec; TechInsights)
