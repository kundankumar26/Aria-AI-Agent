## Lithium Batteries and the Future of EVs
📅 Researched: Thursday 7 May, 2026

**Summary**
Lithium battery tech is accelerating the EV transition through higher energy density, new chemistries like solid-state, and smarter use cases such as second-life for grid storage, while recycling and supply-chain developments shape cost and sustainability.

**Key Findings**
- Solid-state lithium batteries offer higher energy density and safety advantages, with active research and industry interest, though manufacturing scale and cost remain challenges. (Source: The Future of Solid-State Batteries in Electric Vehicles (Zuken US))
- Ultra-fast charging and high-voltage (800V) architectures are being adopted to cut charging times and improve efficiency, a trend highlighted by industry analyses. (Source: Top 10 EV Battery Trends in 2025 and What We Can Expect in 2026 (Calstart); also corroborated by Battery Business Club 2026 trends)
- The second-life EV battery market and recycling sector are expanding rapidly, extending battery life cycles and enabling energy storage applications beyond vehicles. (Source: Second-Life EV Battery Market Size, Trend, Revenue Report 2026 to 2035 (InsightAce Analytic); also discussed in InvRecovery's 2026 playbook)
